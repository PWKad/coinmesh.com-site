About

CoinMesh is open-source and the "go to" start-up kit for Litecoin and Bitcoin app development. By automating the data and adapter layers, developers are empowered to experiment with LTC/BTC development and are able to easily integrate these cryptocurrencies in to their products with just a few clicks.

The minimal initial setup required enables developers to save months of learning that normally is required to leverage blockchain applications effectively. In this way CoinMesh is designed to give developers the jumpstart they need to start testing and validating their products.

The Four Layers of an Application Stack

1

Data

The underlying datastores / databases.

2

Adapter

The layer that enables the applications' logic to interact with the data layer.

3

Logic

The layer for business and application critical logic. Written by your team, runs on your hardware.

4

Application

The layer the client sees in the browser / native app.
